Stochastic Response Surface Method (SRSM)

  • Step 1: Discretize the input random field
    • $$ \begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} E(x,\omega) \approx e_1(x)X_1(\omega) + e_2(x)X_2(\omega) \end{aligned} \end{equation*} $$
  • Step 2: Approximate the output
    • Polynomial chaos with unknown coefficients
      • $$ \begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} w_\xi (\omega) \approx \; & a_0 + \sum_{i=1}^m a_i f_i[X_1(\omega), X_2(\omega)] \\ = \; & a_0 + a_1X_1 + a_2X_2 + a_3(X_1^2-1) + a_4(X_2^2-1) \\ & + a_5X_1X_2 + a_6(X_1^3-3X_1) + a_7(X_2^3-3X_2) + \ldots \end{aligned} \end{equation*} $$
