The proposal

An Integrated Framework for Design for Resilience: From Topology Optimization to Physics-based Design Rule Learning

Fundamental components:

  • Topology optimization
  • Graph-based data mining
  • Resilient design
  • Fault cascading

Three research objectives (RO):

  • RO 1: Reliability-robust topology optimization for nonlinear structures
  • RO 2: Graph representation and resilient-design solutions
  • RO 3: Graph-mining for learning design rules
