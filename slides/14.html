Stochastic Response Surface Method (SRSM)

  • Step 1: Discretize the input random field
    • $$ \begin{equation*} E(x,\omega) \approx e_1(x)X_1(\omega) + e_2(x)X_2(\omega) \end{equation*} $$
  • Step 2: Approximate the output
      • $$ \begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} w_\xi (\omega) \approx \; & a_0 + \sum_{i=1}^m a_i f_i[X_1(\omega), X_2(\omega)] \end{aligned} \end{equation*} $$
  • Step 3: Find unknown coefficients
    • $$ \begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} w_\xi (\omega) = b_k, k = 1,\ldots,m+1 \end{aligned} \end{equation*} $$
